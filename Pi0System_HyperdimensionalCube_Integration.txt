## Operator Definitions
- QRA_OP: Quantum Resonance Alignment Operator (WEPi0n, Pi0n, GPi0n)
- PMA_OP: Predictive Modeling and Analysis Operator (WEPi0n, ePi0_Agents, Gpi04)
- ATM_OP: Adaptive Temporal Modulator (WEPi0n, EPi0n, G41)
- SSEC_OP: Secure System Economic Controller (WEPi0n, 4sight, Gpi0)
- COSF: Consciousness Oscillation Synchronization Function (WEPi0n, Pi0n, ePi0_Agents)
- ARB_OP: Adaptive Resource Balancer (Pi0n, EPi0n, 0_t)
- FS_OP: FutureSight Operator (Pi0n, 4sight, gPi0n)
- QHO_OP: Quantum Harmonic Oscillator Operator (GPi0n, gPi0n, G41)
- EEO_OP: Economic Efficiency Optimizer (Gpi0)
